Gabriela Pauline

Founder & Creative Director

Gabriela Pauline founded Forvoxa with one simple belief:
Every Voice Matters. Every Life Has a Story Worth Remembering.

As Founder and Creative Director, Gabriela leads the creative vision behind every Forvoxa project. She works closely with each client to uncover the defining moments, values, and experiences that have shaped their life, discovering the story that deserves to be remembered. She then guides the creative direction to ensure every song, podcast, music video, and book authentically reflects the person behind the story.

What began as a desire to create meaningful conversations has grown into Forvoxa : a platform dedicated to preserving life’s most meaningful stories through original songs, podcasts, books, and music videos inspired by real human experiences.

A former flight attendant with Singapore Airlines, Gabriela holds a Bachelor of Business from Swinburne University of Technology and an International Master of Management from Universitas Pelita Harapan. Her professional background, education, and experiences as a wife and mother have shaped her passion for understanding people, relationships, and the stories that connect us.

As a lifelong learner, Gabriela continues to explore applied psychology and neuroscience, believing that understanding people is essential to telling stories with authenticity and care. She believes that when people feel truly heard, their stories have the power to encourage others, strengthen families, and become a lasting legacy.

Through Forvoxa, her hope is simple: to create meaningful work that reminds people that every voice matters and every life has a story worth remembering.

“Some of the most meaningful lessons in life are found in the stories we share.”
— Gabriela Pauline

Hosea Hanusia

Music Director & Songwriter

For Hosea Hanusia, every meaningful song begins with a meaningful story.

As Music Director and Songwriter, Hosea transforms real life experiences into original songs that capture the heart, emotion, and authenticity of each journey. Working closely with Gabriela and each client, Hosea helps translate life’s defining moments into melodies and lyrics that feel authentic, timeless, and deeply meaningful.

He believes music is more than something we listen to.

It is something we remember.

A language that connects hearts, preserves memories, and tells stories beyond words.

Fransika Cika

LATEST

Miliki Makna

A meaningful song created to remind the world that every life has meaning.

Our latest focus is “Miliki Makna,” an original song produced by Forvoxa in collaboration with Yayasan Elsafan, a foundation and community for people with visual impairments.

This project reflects one of Forvoxa’s deepest beliefs: that every person has inherent worth, purpose, and a story that deserves to be heard. Through music, we hope to encourage not only people with visual impairments, but also anyone who has ever questioned their value or purpose.

Since its release on digital music platforms, Miliki Makna has touched many lives by carrying a simple yet powerful message:
Your life has meaning.

This project is part of Forvoxa’s ongoing commitment to creating meaningful music and authentic stories that inspire hope, celebrate human dignity, and make a positive impact in our communities.

Because every voice matters.
Because every life has meaning.
