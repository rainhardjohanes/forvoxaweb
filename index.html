TEAM OF FORVOXA

The people behind the stories, music, and impact.

Gabriela Pauline

Founder of Forvoxa

Gabriela Pauline founded Forvoxa with a simple belief: every person has a story worth sharing.

A former flight attendant with Singapore Airlines, Gabriela holds a Bachelor of Business from Swinburne University of Technology and an International Master of Management degree from Universitas Pelita Harapan. Her professional background, education, and experiences as a mother have shaped her passion for understanding people, relationships, and the stories that connect us.

What began as a desire to create meaningful conversations has grown into a platform that brings together stories, music, books, and creative projects inspired by real human experiences. Through Forvoxa, Gabriela explores life’s deeper questions about purpose, relationships, faith, family, and what it means to live a meaningful life.

As a lifelong learner, she is passionate about personal growth, human behavior, and meaningful connection. This interest has led her to further explore applied psychology and neuroscience, seeking a deeper understanding of how people think, feel, relate, and thrive.

Through Forvoxa, she hopes to create work that inspires reflection, encourages deeper conversations, and reminds people that every voice matters and every story carries meaning.

She believes that some of the most powerful moments happen when people feel safe enough to share their truth, and that sometimes a single story can help someone feel less alone.

“Some of the most meaningful lessons in life are found in the stories we share.”
— Gabriela Pauline

Hosea Hanusia

Music Director

Hosea Hanusia is a musician, songwriter, and music director who is deeply passionate about creating music that moves and inspires people. With years of experience in the music industry, he has developed expertise in piano performance, songwriting, music production, arrangement, and audio editing.

At Forvoxa, Hosea helps bring stories to life through music, crafting songs and soundscapes that capture emotion, meaning, and authenticity. His creative vision and dedication to excellence make him an integral part of Forvoxa’s mission to create work of art inspired by real stories.

For Hosea, music is more than a craft. It is a language that connects hearts and tells stories beyond words.

Fransika Cika

Team Coordinator & Video Editor

Fransika Cika brings a unique combination of creativity, organization, and people skills to the Forvoxa team. With a professional background in hospitality, she developed strong communication, coordination, and problem-solving abilities while working in dynamic and fast-paced environments.

At Forvoxa, Cika serves as both Team Coordinator and Video Editor, helping ensure that projects run smoothly from concept to completion. She plays a key role in coordinating team efforts, managing workflows, and transforming stories into engaging visual content that reflects the heart and mission of Forvoxa.

Passionate about meaningful storytelling and creative expression, Cika is committed to helping bring real stories to life in ways that inspire connection, reflection, and purpose.

FORVOXA LEGACY EXPERIENCE™

Transform Your Life Story Into a Meaningful and Timeless Masterpiece

Someday, photos might just be kept in an album.

Videos might be stored away in a folder.

But the stories behind them often slowly fade away.

Stories of life-changing decisions. Stories of struggles that few people see. Stories of prayers once whispered.

Of failures that taught resilience. Of love that isn't always spoken. Of life lessons you hope your loved ones will one day understand.

At Forvoxa, we believe that every life holds a precious story. Not because someone's life is perfect. But because every journey holds meaning. And that meaning is worth passing down.

Through the Forvoxa Legacy Experience™, we help transform meaningful life experiences into original songs, visual storytelling, deep conversations, and a Legacy Book that will live on with your loved ones, both today and in the future.

This is not just a music project. This is a legacy project. A masterpiece born from your life's journey. A way to preserve a story from being lost.

⸻

Imagine This

Imagine one day your children listening to a song inspired by your life's journey.

Not just hearing the music. But understanding the heart behind it. Understanding what you fought for. What you learned. What you believed in. And what you wanted to leave behind for them.

Imagine when they face challenges in life, they can return to listen to your story.

Not because you have all the answers. But because you lived a real life. With real challenges. With real fears. With real hope.

And perhaps, through that story, they find the strength to keep moving forward.

Because sometimes the greatest legacy we leave behind is not what we own. It is what we learned. Who we loved. And how we lived our lives.

⸻

More Than Just a Story

The Forvoxa Legacy Experience™ is not just about documenting the past. It’s about discovering the meaning behind your life's journey and transforming it into something that will live on.

Everyone has a story. Some are full of joy. Some are full of challenges. Some may have never truly been told to anyone.

But often, it is within those very experiences that the most valuable lessons reside. About love. About loss. About forgiveness. About the courage to rise again. About faith when things don't go as expected. About how someone still chooses to walk forward even when the path isn't easy.

When these stories are shared, they have the power to strengthen others. To make someone feel less alone. To give hope. To remind us that a meaningful life is not a perfect life. It is a life lived wholeheartedly.

Through music, stories, and meaningful conversations, we help transform your life experiences into a masterpiece that will continue to inspire the people you love. Not just as a memory. But as a message. Not just as a story. But as a legacy.

⸻

Who Is This Program For?

The Forvoxa Legacy Experience™ is designed for individuals, couples, parents, families, leaders, business founders, and anyone who has a meaningful story they want to share.

You don't have to be a singer. You don't have to have musical experience. You don't need to have an extraordinary story. You just need to have a story that matters to you.

Because often, the most touching stories are not about massive achievements. They are stories about real life. About struggles. About love. About family. About hope. About being human.

Some clients choose to sing their own songs. Some choose to participate in the creative process. Others choose to share their story and entrust professional singers to perform the song. Whatever the choice, the story remains yours.

⸻

What Will You Get?

Two Original Songs

Two songs professionally written and produced based on your life experiences, values, message, and personal journey. Each song is created specifically and exclusively for you. No templates. No identical songs. Because no two lives have the same story.

Collaborative Songwriting Process

We take the time to listen. To understand. To dig into the moments that mean the most in your life. Together, we will discover the story you want to share and transform it into an authentic musical piece.

Professional Music Production

Arrangement. Recording. Production. Mixing. Mastering. Handled professionally by our creative team.

Music Video Production

A professional music video that helps bring your story to life in a meaningful visual format.

Podcast Feature

A dedicated podcast session to share the story, meaning, values, and life lessons behind the created song. Because sometimes the story behind a song is just as precious as the song itself.

Legacy Book

A digital Legacy Book designed to summarize key moments, reflections, values, and life lessons from your journey. This book typically consists of 30–50 pages and serves as a companion to the song and podcast. For clients who wish to expand their story into a full book, advanced development options are available separately.

Global Digital Release

Your song will be released on various digital platforms such as Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and other streaming platforms. So that the people you love can access it whenever they need it.

A Meaningful Legacy

A collection of works designed to live on and inspire for years to come.

⸻

Created by Humans, Not Algorithms

At Forvoxa, we believe some stories deserve a truly personal touch.

Every lyric. Every melody. Every conversation. Every creative decision. Crafted by humans who take the time to understand your story.

We do not use artificial intelligence for songwriting, music production, vocals, or video creation as part of our core creative process. Because we believe human stories deserve to be treated humanely. And every story deserves genuine attention.

⸻

The Creative Journey

Stage 1: Getting to Know Your Story

We listen to your life journey, experiences, values, and the message you want to share.

Stage 2: Song Creation

Our team begins developing original songs inspired by your story.

Stage 3: Production

The song is recorded, produced, mixed, and mastered professionally.

Stage 4: Video, Podcast & Legacy Book

We help present your story through a music video, podcast, and a custom-designed Legacy Book.

Stage 5: Launch

Your masterpiece is prepared and released to various digital platforms.

⸻

Project Duration

Every story has a different journey. Most projects take about 3 to 6 months from the initial stage to launch.

Meaningful work takes time. Attention. Sincerity. And an unhurried process.
